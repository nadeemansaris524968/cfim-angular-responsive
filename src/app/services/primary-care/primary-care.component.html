Primary Care Q & A

What is primary care?

Primary care is medical care that provides a foundation for health and wellness. It aims to establish a reliable structure of well-being to support your passions, pursuits, and relationships in life so you can get the most out of every day.

As your primary care provider, Dr. Ansari helps you prevent illness in the short- and long-term, answer questions as they arise, and manage chronic diseases. A good relationship with a trusted primary care provider can mean a healthier, longer life for you and your family.

Am I old enough to look for a primary care provider?

The pursuit of health doesn’t have a qualifying age. Regardless of your age, gender, or lifestyle, anyone can benefit from having a trusted primary care provider to understand your needs and customize your care as you move through the seasons of life.

What primary care services can I expect?

At California Functional Internal Medicine Clinic, Dr. Ansari and the team don’t just look at your symptoms. Instead, they examine your health from a broader perspective to find the root cause of ailments and address or correct a wide variety of health conditions, such as:

  • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Diabetes
  • Cholesterol issues
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Food intolerances/Gut issues
  • Mold Toxicity
  • Chronic Lyme disease

Book an appointment online if you are in need of:

Wellness exams

A wellness exam is an excellent opportunity to touch base with Dr. Ansari and the team, ask about your health, and get an up-to-date assessment of where you stand. Wellness exams are especially crucial for preventing chronic illnesses.

Annual physicals

Like a wellness exam, annual physicals track your health, but these visits occur at specific intervals. This type of comprehensive evaluation can be your first warning of any potential health concerns.

Sick visits

If you’re under the weather, get in touch with Dr. Ansari for quick care and helpful advice.

Follow up visits

Once you’ve received a treatment plan, the team at California Functional Internal Medicine Clinic wants to ensure you’re getting the best results from your care. To this end, follow-up visits track your progress and allow Dr. Ansari to adjust your care as needed.

Dr. Ansari can also offer sound medical guidance for weight loss, allergies, or recovery from mold toxicity just to name a few.
